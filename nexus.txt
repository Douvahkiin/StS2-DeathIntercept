[size=4][b]English[/b][/size]

[b]What is this?[/b]

DeathIntercept is a mod for Slay the Spire 2 that lets you choose whether to retry a combat or give up when your character dies. Instead of immediately ending your run and deleting the auto-save, the mod pauses the death flow and presents a dialog:

[list]
[*] [b]Retry[/b] (Enter) — Reload the combat directly from the pre-combat save. The fight restarts from round 1.
[*] [b]Give Up[/b] (Esc) — Proceed with the normal game over flow. Saves your score, badges, and run history.
[/list]

[b]How it works[/b]

[list=1]
[*] Intercepts [code]CreatureCmd.Kill[/code] before the death music and game-over logic run.
[*] Preserves the run save by blocking [code]SaveManager.DeleteCurrentRun[/code].
[*] On retry, reloads the combat in-place using the same flow as the main menu "Continue" button — no main menu transition.
[/list]

[b]Installation[/b]

[list=1]
[*] Drop [code]DeathIntercept.dll[/code] and [code]DeathIntercept.json[/code] into [code]Slay the Spire 2/mods/DeathIntercept/[/code].
[*] Launch the game. The mod auto-activates.
[/list]

[b]Credits[/b]

The combat reload logic is adapted from [url=https://github.com/mmmmie/STS2-QuickReload]STS2-QuickReload[/url], specifically the [code]QuickReloadRunner.RestartSinglePlayer()[/code] flow. Thank you for the reference implementation!

[size=4][b]汉语[/b][/size]

[b]这是什么？[/b]

DeathIntercept 是《杀戮尖塔2》的一个 Mod，让你在角色阵亡时可以选择重打或放弃。不再直接结束对局并删除自动存档，而是暂停死亡流程，弹出对话框：

[list]
[*] [b]重打[/b]（Enter）—— 从战斗前存档直接重载战斗，从第一回合重新开始。
[*] [b]放弃[/b]（Esc）—— 走正常游戏结束流程，保存分数、徽章和对局历史。
[/list]

[b]原理[/b]

[list=1]
[*] 在战败 BGM 和游戏结束逻辑运行[b]之前[/b]拦截 [code]CreatureCmd.Kill[/code]。
[*] 通过拦截 [code]SaveManager.DeleteCurrentRun[/code] 保护战斗前存档不被删除。
[*] 选择重打时，复用主菜单「继续」按钮的流程原地重载战斗——无需返回主菜单，无需额外点击。
[/list]

[b]安装[/b]

[list=1]
[*] 将 [code]DeathIntercept.dll[/code] 和 [code]DeathIntercept.json[/code] 放入 [code]Slay the Spire 2/mods/DeathIntercept/[/code]。
[*] 启动游戏，Mod 自动激活。
[/list]

[b]鸣谢[/b]

战斗重载逻辑参考了 [url=https://github.com/mmmmie/STS2-QuickReload]STS2-QuickReload[/url]，尤其是 [code]QuickReloadRunner.RestartSinglePlayer()[/code] 的实现。感谢参考代码！
